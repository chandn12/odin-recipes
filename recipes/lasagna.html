Lamb Lasagna

Description

This Delicious Lamb Lasagna is one of my favourite meals

Ingredients

White/Béchamel Sauce

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 cups milk
    • 3
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 small onion cut into big chunks salt
  • 6 peppercorns

Meat Sauce

  • 500 gram lamb mince
  • 2 bay leaf
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 2 tspn minced garlic
  • 1 tspn fresh thyme
  • 2 tspn Robertson's dried Italian herbs
  • 1/2 tspn dried oregano
  • 1&1/2 tspn chilli powder
  • 4 roma tomatoes pureed, makes about 1&1/2 cups
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 mutton stock cube/bouillon
  • 1/2 cup water
  • salt and pepper
  • 1&1/2 cup cheddar cheese
  • 1&1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • little sprinkle of paprika

Steps

White/Béchamel Sauce

  1. Add the peppercorns, bay leaf and onion to the milk. Simmer on low heat until it comes to a boil. Allow it to stand for 15 minutes. Strain and set aside.
  2. Melt butter on medium heat. Add flour and cook until it thickens
  3. Slowly add the milk and whisk until smooth.
  4. Simmer until sauce thickens. Add a little more milk if sauce is too thick. Season with salt and pepper and set aside

Meat Sauce

  1. Brown the mince on high heat. Add the bay leaf and onion and sauté until translucent
  2. Add the thyme and garlic, sauté for another minute
  3. Add the Italian herbs, chilli powder and tomato paste. Mix well and cook for 30 seconds
  4. Add blended tomatoes. Mix the stock cube with half cup water and mix it in. Add some pepper a pinch of sugar if the sauce is too acidic
  5. Simmer on low heat for at least an hour until the tomatoes are cooked and sauce thickens. You can add a little water if the sauce dries up and continue cooking. Only add more salt if required.

Layering the Lasagna

  1. Add 1 cup of white sauce at the bottom of an ovenproof dish. Mix the mozzarella and cheddar and set aside
  2. Place 3 of the cooked lasagna sheets on top
  3. Add 1/3 of the mince sauce. Sprinkle some cheese over
  4. Add another layer of lasagna. Another some of the white sauce.
  5. Add 1/3 of the mince sauce. Sprinkle some cheese over
  6. Place 3 of the lasagna sheets on top
  7. Add another layer of the mince sauce. Add more white sauce and some cheese on top. Place the last 3 lasagna sheets on top
  8. Add the last bit of mince on top of the lasagna sheets. Pour the remaining sauce over together with the leftover cheese. Sprinkle some paprika over the cheese, (pop some toothpicks on top, this will prevent the foil from sticking to the cheese) cover with foil and bake in the oven at 180 degrees Celsius until lasagna it heats through and cheese melts, about 15 minutes. Cook longer if required. In the last 5 minutes you an turn on the grill function and remove the foil
  9. Allow it to rest for 15 minutes before serving
